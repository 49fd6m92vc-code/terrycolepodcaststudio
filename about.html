About the Studio

The Terry Cole Podcast Studio is part of the Terry Cole Broadcast & Recording Studio, created to preserve, share, and continue a legacy rooted in radio, storytelling, and community service.

Named in honor of broadcaster Terry Cole, the studio serves as both an archival space and a living production environment — connecting past broadcasts with new voices and contemporary audio projects.

This studio exists to ensure meaningful voices, local history, and broadcast craftsmanship are preserved with care and intention.

In addition to archival work, the studio supports podcasts and recorded projects from community members, institutions, and collaborators who share a commitment to thoughtful, purpose-driven audio.
